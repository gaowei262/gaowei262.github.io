Biography

    Wei GAO is an Assistant Professor/Ph.D. Supervisor/PI at the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Peking University, China (Since 2019). He is now leading the Multimedia Computing and AI Lab (MMCAL). His research interests include Multimedia Computing and Artificial Intelligence, especially 3D Visual Data Coding, Processing, and Applications. He is a Senior Member of IEEE.

    Research: He has published over 180 research papers, applied over 100 patents, and submitted over 70 standard proposals. As the first recipient, he received the Second Prize of the Natural Science Award of the China Society of Image and Graphics in 2024 (Theories and Methods for Multimodal Visual Perception with Restricted Environments, Ranked 1). He was a recipient of the IEEE Multimedia Rising Star (Runner Up Award) for Outstanding Early-stage Career Achievements in the Area of 3D Immersive Media Research in 2021, and received the China Computer Federation Tencent Rhino Bird Excellent Patent Award in 2021. He authored two books published by Springer Nature, including “Deep Learning for 3D Point Clouds” and “Point Cloud Compression: Technologies and Standardization”, and received the 2025 Springer Nature - China New Development Awards. He participated in the drafting of China's AI series white paper on deep learning in 2023. He is actively participating in the development of multimedia and artificial intelligence standards. He participated in the establishment of group standards for “Information Technology - High Efficiency Graphics Data Coding, Part 2: Point Clouds” and “Information Technology - High Efficiency Graphics Data Coding, Part 3: Subjective Quality Assessment Methods for Point Clouds”. He received the 2023 AVS Industrial Technology Innovation Team Award (For Contributions to Point Cloud Compression Standard). He established several open source projects, including OpenPointCloud (3D Point Cloud Coding and Processing), OpenAICoding (Deep Learning-based Image/Video Coding), OpenDatasets (Large-scale Datasets for Multimedia Computing and AI), OpenAIDriving (AI-based Autonomous Driving). He received the China Computer Federation Excellent Open Source Graphics Software Award in 2022. He received 2 Outstanding Paper Awards from the Guangdong Computer Federation in 2019/2018, and 5 Outstanding Paper Awards from the Shenzhen Science and Technology Association in 2024/2023/2022. He has undertaken more than 20 government-funded and industry-funded research projects, including National Key R&D Program, National Natural Science Foundation, Guangdong Provincial Natural Science Foundation, Shenzhen City Natural Science Foundation.

    Teaching: He has been teaching two courses to Ph.D. and M.Phil. students at Peking University since 2019, including “3D Vision & Computational Photography” and “Selected Topics in Advanced Video Processing”. His course “3D Vision & Computational Photography” was admitted to Peking University Graduate Course Construction Funding Project in 2024, and was admitted to the Shared Course Program of the University Town of Shenzhen. He was the recipient of the Excellent Award in the Teaching Basic Skills Competition for Young Teachers at Peking University in 2024. He has supervised or is supervising more than 40 Ph.D. and M.Phil. graduate students, and they have received many distinguished awards and honors, including the National Scholarship, Excellent Graduate of Beijing City, Excellent Graduate of Peking University, Merit Student Elite of Peking University, and 2024 Shenzhen Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award for Artificial Intelligence.

    Service: He is an Elected Member of IEEE Multimedia Systems and Applications Technical Committee (IEEE MSA-TC), an Elected Member of IEEE Visual Signal Processing and Communications Technical Committee (IEEE VSPC-TC), and an Elected Member of APSIPA Image Video and Multimedia Technical Committee (APSIPA IVM-TC). He is an Elected Member of China Computer Federation Technical Committee on Multimedia Technology (CCF TCMM), and an Elected Member of China Society of Image and Graphics Technical Committee on Multimedia (CSIG TCMM), CSIG Technical Committee on 3D Vision (CSIG TC3DV), CSIG Technical Committee on Image and Video Communications (CSIG TCIVC). He is currently Associate Editor for ACM Transactions on Multimedia Computing, Communications, and Applications (ACM TOMM), Elsevier Signal Processing (Elsevier SIGPRO), Springer Neural Processing Letters (Springer NEPL), APSIPA Transactions on Signal and Information Processing (APSIPA SIP). He was the Guest Editor for Special Issue on Deep Learning-Based Data Compression in APSIPA Transactions on Signal and Information Processing in 2024, and Special Issue on 3D Point Cloud Processing and Applications in ZTE Communications in 2023. He organized workshops and special sessions at ACM MM 2025, IEEE ICME 2023, ACM MM 2022, IEEE VCIP 2022, and IEEE ICME 2021. He was the tutorial speaker at ACM MM 2025, ACM MM 2024, IEEE ICIP 2024, IEEE IJCNN 2024, and IEEE ICME 2023. He has served as the reviewer of NSFC funds and other project funds. He has served as the reviewer for international top journals including IEEE TPAMI, TIP, TVCG, TCSVT, TMM, TNNLS, as well as international top conferences including NeurIPS, CVPR, ECCV, AAAI, ACM MM, IJCAI, ICLR. He has served as Area Chair and Technical Program Committee Member for international conferences.

    Lab Goal: He founded the MMCAL Lab in 2019, and is now leading the research team to work on the advanced technologies of immersive media and 3D vision. The research team focuses on emerging 3D visual intelligence technologies, especially 3D visual data coding, processing, and applications, for the benefits of both human and machine. The research team has extensive research cooperations with the industry to promote the applications of related technologies in the field of intelligent 3D visual data coding and processing.

    高伟，北京大学信息工程学院助理教授/研究员/博士生导师/PI（2019年起）。北京大学多媒体计算与智能实验室（MMCAL）负责人。北京大学视频与视觉技术国家工程研究中心（深圳）成员。入选广东省青年拔尖人才、深圳市孔雀计划高层次人才。长期从事多媒体计算与人工智能领域研究，特别是3D视觉数据编码、处理与应用。研究方向主要包括：（1）3D视觉数据编码：3D视觉数据编码（3D点云编码、3D Gaussian Splatting压缩/渲染、2D/3D图像视频编码、深度学习智能编码、人机感知共友好编码）、3D视觉数据质量评价与增强、智能编码标准；（2）3D视觉数据处理与应用：3D重建生成与3D感知理解（3D Gaussian Splatting重建/生成/表征）、面向空间智能的3D视觉与多模态学习（生成式人工智能/世界模型、视觉-语言-动作多模态大模型）、沉浸交互媒体通信（VR/AR/MR）与自主智能无人系统（自动驾驶/机器人）应用。此外，还关注显著性分割、模型压缩与加速、FPGA/ASIC与嵌入式系统、开源项目等研究问题。IEEE/CCF/CSIG Senior Member。

Research

    We are working on Multimedia/Multimodal Information Processing, especially 3D Visual Data Coding, Processing, and Applications (immersive media communication and autonomous unmanned systems).
  • 3D Visual Data Coding:
    3D Visual Data Coding (3D Point Cloud Coding, 3D Gaussian Splatting Compression/Rendering, 2D/3D Image/Video Coding, Deep Learning-Based Coding, Human and Machine Perception-Driven Coding)
    3D Visual Data Quality Assessment and Enhancement
    Developments of AI-based Coding Standards
  • 3D Visual Data Processing and Applications:
    3D Reconstruction/Generation and 3D Perception/Understanding (3D Gaussian Splatting Reconstruction/Generation/Representation)
    3D Vision and Multimodal Learning for Spatial Intelligence (Generative AI/World Models, Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Models)
    Applications of Immersive Interactive Media Communication (VR/AR/MR) and Autonomous Intelligent Unmanned Systems (Autonomous Driving/Robotics)
Other Research Topics
  • Saliency Segmentation for RGB-D/RGB-T/Light Field/Point Cloud (See TPAMI 2023: A Thorough Benchmark and A New Model for Light Field Saliency Detection)
  • Model Compression and Acceleration (See IJCV 2024: Interpretable Task-inspired Adaptive Filter Pruning for Neural Networks Under Multiple Constraints)
  • FPGA/ASIC and Embedded Systems (See ACM MM 2023: OpenHardwareVC: An Open Source Library for 8K UHD Video Coding Hardware Implementation)
  • Open Source Projects (See ACM MM 2022: OpenPointCloud: An Open-Source Algorithm Library of Deep Learning Based Point Cloud Compression)
