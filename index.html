Biography

    Dr. Wei GAO is an Assistant Professor/Researcher/Ph.D. Supervisor with the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Shenzhen Graduate School, Peking University (PKU), Shenzhen, China. His research has been focused on perception-inspired multimedia coding and processing, including both efficient algorithms and systems. His research interests include Multimedia Coding and Processing (image/video, point cloud, light field, etc.), and the related topics of Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence (efficient AI). He won the 2021 IEEE Multimedia Rising Star (Runner Up Award) for Outstanding Early-stage Career Achievements in the Area of 3D Immersive Media Research. He is currently serving or has served as Associate Editor for several international journals on multimedia computing and machine leaning, including Signal Processing (Elsevier), Neural Processing Letters (Springer), IET Image Processing, IET Electronics Letters. He is also an Elected Member of Visual Signal Processing and Communications Technical Committee, IEEE Circuits and Systems Society (IEEE CASS VSPC-TC), and an Elected Member of Image, Video, and Multimedia Technical Committee, Asia-Pacific Signal and Information Processing Association (APSIPA IVM-TC). He is a Guest Editor for Special Issue on 3D Point Cloud Processing and Applications at ZTE Communications. He has organized workshops and special sessions at IEEE ICME 2023, ACM MM 2022, IEEE VCIP 2022, and IEEE ICME 2021. He is a tutorial speaker for point cloud related topics at IEEE ICIP 2024 and IEEE ICME 2023. He is also the leader to establish several open source projects, including OpenPointCloud (Open Source Project for Point Cloud Coding and Processing), OpenHardwareVC (Open Source Project for AVS3 8K Encoding Hardware), OpenAICoding (Open Source Project for Learning-based Image/Video Coding with Multi-Platform Support), OpenCompression (Open Source Project for Visual Media Compression Optimization), OpenVision (Open Source Project for Computer Vision), and OpenDatasets (Large-scale Datasets for Multimedia Computing and AI). He is now leading an active research team working on advanced technologies of immersive and 3D visual media processing. The research team endeavors to improve the visual experience and industrial applications of immersive and 3D visual media, and thus boost the advancements of emerging and future multimedia and visual information processing technology. He is a Senior Member of IEEE.

Research

    My research interests focus on Multimedia Coding and Processing, and the related topics of Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence.
  • Multimedia Coding:
    Point Cloud Compression and 2D/3D/Perceptual Video Coding, AI-Based Visual Media Compression (Immersive and 3D Visual Media: Point Cloud, Mesh, Light Field, Panorama, Multiview/Binocular 3D)
  • Multimedia Processing and Computer Vision:
    Point Cloud Processing and Analysis, Image/Video Processing and Analysis, Immersive and 3D Visual Media Processing and Analysis (Quality Assessment and Saliency Segmentation, Enhancement/Restoration and Machine Analysis, Multimodal Information Fusion)
  • Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence:
    Multimodal Big Model/AIGC/Embodied AI/Trustworthy AI, Light-weight Deep Neural Networks and Software/Hardware (VLSI) Implementation, Open Source Projects
