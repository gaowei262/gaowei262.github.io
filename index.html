Biography

    Dr. Wei GAO is an Assistant Professor/Researcher/PhD Supervisor with the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Shenzhen Graduate School, Peking University (PKU), Shenzhen, China. He is now leading an active research team working on advanced technologies of immersive media processing. He received the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), Kowloon, Hong Kong. He was an Camera ISP Engineer at OmniVision Technologies (OVT), Shanghai, China. He was a Visiting Scholar at the Electrical Engineering Department, University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), CA, USA. He worked as Postdoctoral Fellow and Research Associate at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), Kowloon, Hong Kong, and Research Fellow at the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, respectively. His research interests include Multimedia Coding and Processing, and the related topics of Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence. Moreover, theoretical research on applied mathematics and optimization, visual perception, aritifical intelligence and practical information systems are also very interesting. He believes that: good innovations should have good explainable theories. He won the IEEE Multimedia Rising Star (Runner Up Award) in 2021. He is currently serving as Associate Editor for four international journals on machine leaning and multimedia computing, including Signal Processing (Elsevier), Neural Processing Letters (Springer), IET Image Processing, Electronics Letters. He is also an Elected Member of Image, Video, and Multimedia Technical Committee, Asia-Pacific Signal and Information Processing Association (APSIPA IVM TC). He has organized workshops and special sessions at IEEE ICME 2021, IEEE VCIP 2022 and ACM MM 2022. He is also the leader to establish several open source projects on OpenI platform, including OpenPointCloud (First Open Source Project for Point Cloud Coding and Processing), OpenHardwareVC (First Open Source Project for AVS3 8K Encoding Hardware), OpenAICoding, OpenCompression, OpenVision, and OpenDatasets. The research team endeavors to improve the visual experience and industrial applications of immersive and 3D visual media, and thus boost the advancements of emerging and future multimedia and visual information processing technology. He is a Senior Member of IEEE.

Research

    My research interests focus on Multimedia Coding and Processing, and the related topics of Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence.
  • Multimedia Coding and Communication:
    Point Cloud Compression and 2D/3D/Perceptual Video Coding, AI-Based Visual Media Compression, Immersive and 3D Visual Media Communication (Point Cloud, Light Field, Panorama, Multiview/Binocular 3D Visual Media Streaming and Transmission for VR/AR)
  • Multimedia Processing and Analysis:
    Point Cloud Processing and Analysis, Image/Video Processing and Analysis, Immersive and 3D Visual Media Processing and Analysis (Quality Assessment and Saliency Segmentation, Enhancement/Restoration and Multidimensional Analysis)
  • Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence:
    Multimedia and AI System Implementation (HW/SW Accelerators and VLSI/Embedded Hardware Design, Light-weight Deep Neural Networks and Neural Architecture Search, Open Source Projects), Interpretable/Distributed Deep Learning (Big Models and Pre-training Models)
    Our current ongoing projects are as follows:
  • Point Cloud Compression Standardization and Deep Learning Based Point Cloud Coding
  • 2D/3D Video Coding Standardization and Deep Learning Based Image/Video Coding
  • Immersive and 3D Visual Media Processing/Analysis and Applications
