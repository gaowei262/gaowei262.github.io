Biography

Wei Gao received the Ph.D. degree in Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong, Kowloon, Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Sam Kwong. From February 2012 to August 2013, he was a full-time Camera Image Signal Processor (ISP) Engineer in OmniVision Technologies, Shanghai, China. Before that, he recieved the M.S. degree in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems from Institute for Pattern Recognition and Artificial Intelligence (National Key Laboratory of Science and Technology on Multi-spectral Information Processing), Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China, in 2012. From January to June 2016, he was a Visiting Scholar in Electrical Engineering Department, University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), CA, USA. From January to March 2017, he was a Postdoctoral Fellow in Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong, Kowloon, Hong Kong. Currently, he is a Research Fellow in School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, working with Prof. Weisi Lin.

Research Interests

Currently, I am interested in diverse topics on multimedia signal processing and machine learning with emphasis on:
[1] 2D/3D video coding, processing and transmission/streaming (H.265/HEVC and beyond);
[2] Perceptual image/video processing: visual attention, just-noticeable-distortion, image/video quality assessment and visual quality-of-experience;
[3] Video content processing, analysis and understanding;
[4] Emerging multimedia signal processing topics;
[5] Machine learning theory and advanced optimization techniques.
Moreover, I also have hands-on experiences in high-performance VLSI design for multimedia and signal processing.
