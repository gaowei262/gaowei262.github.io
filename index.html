Biography

    Wei Gao is currently a Tenured Associate Professor/Ph.D. Supervisor/PI at the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Peking University, China. He is now leading the Multimedia Computing and AI Lab (MMCAL). His research interests include Multimedia Computing and Artificial Intelligence, especially 3D Visual Intelligence, Multimodal Intelligence, and Applications (Immersive Media, Autonomous Driving/Robotics). He is a Senior Member of IEEE.

    Research: He has published over 200 research papers in international journals (IEEE TPAMI/TIP/TCSVT/TMM/TNNLS/TGRS and IJCV, etc.) and international conferences (CVPR/ICCV/ECCV/ACM MM/AAAI/NeurIPS/ICLR, etc.). He authored four books published by Springer Nature, including ÔÇťAI-based 3D Point Cloud CodingÔÇŁ, ÔÇťAI-based Image and Video CodingÔÇŁ, ÔÇťDeep Learning for 3D Point CloudsÔÇŁ, and ÔÇťPoint Cloud CompressionÔÇŁ, and received the 2025 Springer Nature - China New Development Awards. He participated in the drafting of China's AI series white paper on deep learning in 2023. He has applied over 80 US/China patents (40+ Granted), and submitted over 80 MPEG/AVS standard proposals (30+ Adopted). He is actively participating in the development of multimedia and artificial intelligence standards. He participated in the establishment of group standards for ÔÇťInformation Technology - High Efficiency Graphics Data Coding, Part 2: Point CloudsÔÇŁ and ÔÇťInformation Technology - High Efficiency Graphics Data Coding, Part 3: Subjective Quality Assessment Methods for Point CloudsÔÇŁ. He is one of the key contributors to the first and second generation China point cloud compression standards. He received the 2023 AVS Industrial Technology Innovation Team Award (For Contributions to Point Cloud Compression Standard). He established several open source projects, including OpenPointCloud (Point Cloud Coding and Processing), OpenAICoding (Deep Learning-based Image/Video Coding), OpenAIDriving (AI-based Autonomous Driving), OpenDatasets (Datasets for Multimedia Computing and AI). He received the China Computer Federation Excellent Open Source Graphics Software Award in 2022. He was selected to Stanford University ÔÇťThe WorldÔÇÖs Top 2% ScientistsÔÇŁ List. He received the Second Prize of the Natural Science Award of the China Society of Image and Graphics in 2024 (Theories and Methods for Multimodal Visual Perception with Restricted Environments, as the First Recipient). He was a recipient of IEEE Visual Signal Processing and Communications (VSPC) Rising Star (Honorable Mention Award) in 2025. He was a recipient of the IEEE Multimedia Rising Star (Runner Up Award) for Outstanding Early-stage Career Achievements in the Area of 3D Immersive Media Research in 2021. He received the China Computer Federation Tencent Rhino Bird Excellent Patent Award in 2021. He was a recipient of the IEEE CASS Multimedia Systems and Applications Technical Committee (MSA-TC) Best Paper Award in 2026 (As the Corresponding Author). He has undertaken more than 20 government-funded and industry-funded research projects, including National Key R&D Program, National Natural Science Foundation, Guangdong Provincial Natural Science Foundation, Shenzhen City Natural Science Foundation.

    Teaching: He has been teaching two courses to Ph.D. and M.Phil. students at Peking University since 2019, including ÔÇť3D Vision & Computational PhotographyÔÇŁ and ÔÇťSelected Topics in Advanced Video ProcessingÔÇŁ. His course ÔÇť3D Vision & Computational PhotographyÔÇŁ was admitted to Peking University Graduate Course Construction Funding Project in 2024, and was admitted to the Shared Course Program of the University Town of Shenzhen. He has supervised or is supervising more than 50 Ph.D. and M.Phil. graduate students, and they have received many distinguished awards and honors, including the National Scholarship, Excellent Graduate of Beijing City, Excellent Graduate of Peking University, Merit Student Elite of Peking University, and 2024 Shenzhen Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award for Artificial Intelligence, 2026 IEEE CASS Multimedia Systems and Applications Technical Committee (MSA-TC) Best Paper Award.

    Service: He is an Elected Member of IEEE Multimedia Systems and Applications Technical Committee (IEEE MSA-TC), an Elected Member of IEEE Visual Signal Processing and Communications Technical Committee (IEEE VSPC-TC), and an Elected Member of APSIPA Image Video and Multimedia Technical Committee (APSIPA IVM-TC). He is an Elected Member of China Computer Federation Technical Committee on Multimedia Technology (CCF TCMM), and an Elected Member of China Society of Image and Graphics Technical Committee on Multimedia (CSIG TCMM), CSIG Technical Committee on 3D Vision (CSIG TC3DV), CSIG Technical Committee on Image and Video Communications (CSIG TCIVC). He is currently Associate Editor for IEEE Transactions on Image Processing (IEEE TIP), IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology (IEEE TCSVT), IEEE Transactions on Multimedia (IEEE TMM). He was the Guest Editor for Special Issue on Deep Learning-Based Data Compression in APSIPA Transactions on Signal and Information Processing in 2024, and Special Issue on 3D Point Cloud Processing and Applications in ZTE Communications in 2023. He organized workshops and special sessions at ACM MM 2025, IEEE ICME 2023, ACM MM 2022, IEEE VCIP 2022, and IEEE ICME 2021. He was the tutorial speaker at ACM MM 2025, ACM MM 2024, IEEE ICIP 2024, IEEE IJCNN 2024, and IEEE ICME 2023. He has served as the reviewer for international top journals including IEEE TPAMI, TIP, TVCG, TCSVT, TMM, TNNLS, as well as international top conferences including NeurIPS, CVPR, ECCV, AAAI, ACM MM, IJCAI, ICLR. He has served as Area Chair for international top conferences. He has served as the reviewer of NSFC funds and other project funds.

    Lab Goal: He founded MMCAL in 2019, and is now leading the research team to work on the advanced technologies of immersive media and 3D vision. The research team aims to promote the developments of emerging 3D visual intelligence technologies for the benefits of both human and machine perception. The key application fields include immersive interactive media communication (VR/AR/MR) and autonomous intelligent unmanned systems (autonomous driving/robotics). The research team has extensive research collaborations with the industry to promote the applications of related technologies in the field of intelligent 3D visual data processing.

Research

    We are focusing on Multimedia Computing and Artificial Intelligence, especially 3D Visual Intelligence, Multimodal Intelligence, and Applications.
  • Intelligent 3D Visual Data Coding and Processing:
    3D Visual Data Coding (3D Point Cloud Coding, 3D Gaussian Splatting Compression/Rendering, 2D/3D Image/Video Coding, Deep Learning-Based Coding, Human and Machine Perception-Driven Coding)
    3D Visual Data Quality Assessment and Enhancement
    AI-based Coding Standards and Transmission Systems

  • Spatial Intelligence and Embodied Intelligence:
    Generative AI/World Models for 3D Reconstruction/Generation
    Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Models for 3D Perception/Understanding
    3D Vision and Multimodal Learning for Spatial Intelligence and Embodied Intelligence
    We aim to conduct in-depth research on the theories and methods of 3D visual intelligence and multimodal intelligence, and promote the application of related technologies in immersive interactive media communication (VR/AR/MR) and autonomous intelligent unmanned systems (autonomous driving/robotics).
Other Research Topics
  • Saliency Segmentation for RGB-D/RGB-T/Light Field/Point Cloud (See TPAMI 2023: A Thorough Benchmark and A New Model for Light Field Saliency Detection)
  • Model Compression and Acceleration (See IJCV 2024: Interpretable Task-inspired Adaptive Filter Pruning for Neural Networks Under Multiple Constraints)
  • FPGA/ASIC and Embedded Systems (See ACM MM 2023: OpenHardwareVC: An Open Source Library for 8K UHD Video Coding Hardware Implementation)
  • Open Source Projects (See ACM MM 2022: OpenPointCloud: An Open-Source Algorithm Library of Deep Learning Based Point Cloud Compression)
