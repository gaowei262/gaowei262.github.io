Biography

    Dr. Wei GAO is an Assistant Professor/Researcher/PhD Supervisor with the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Shenzhen Graduate School, Peking University (PKU), Shenzhen, China. He is now leading an active research team working on advanced technologies of immersive media processing. He received the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), Kowloon, Hong Kong. He was an Camera ISP Engineer at OmniVision Technologies (OVT), Shanghai, China. He was a Visiting Scholar at the Electrical Engineering Department, University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), CA, USA. He worked as Postdoctoral Fellow and Research Associate at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), Kowloon, Hong Kong, and Research Fellow at the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, respectively. His research interests include Multimedia Coding, Multimedia Processing and Computer Vision, and Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence. Moreover, theoretical research on applied mathematics and optimization, visual perception, aritifical intelligence and practical information systems are also very interesting. He believes that: good innovations should have good explainable theories. He won the IEEE Multimedia Rising Star (Runner Up Award) in 2021. He is currently serving as Associate Editor for four international journals on machine leaning and multimedia computing, including Signal Processing (Elsevier), Neural Processing Letters (Springer), IET Image Processing, Electronics Letters. He is also an Elected Member of Image, Video, and Multimedia Technical Committee, Asia-Pacific Signal and Information Processing Association (APSIPA IVM TC). He has organized workshops and special sessions at IEEE ICME 2021, IEEE VCIP 2022 and ACM MM 2022. He is also the leader to establish several open source projects on OpenI platform, including OpenPointCloud (First Open Source Project for Point Cloud Coding and Processing), OpenHardwareVC (First Open Source Project for AVS3 8K Encoding Hardware), OpenAICoding, OpenCompression, OpenVision, and OpenDatasets. The research team endeavors to improve the visual experience and industrial applications of immersive and 3D visual media, and thus boost the advancements of emerging and future multimedia and visual information processing technology. He is a Senior Member of IEEE.

Research

    Our research areas are Multimedia Coding, Multimedia Processing and Computer Vision, and Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence.
  • Multimedia Coding:
    Point Cloud Compression, 2D/3D/Perceptual Video Coding, AI-Based Media Compression for Immersive and 3D Visual Media Communication (including Point Cloud, Light Field, Panorama, Multiview/Binocular 3D for VR/AR)
  • Multimedia Processing and Computer Vision:
    Point Cloud Processing and Analysis, Image/Video Processing and Analysis, Computer Vision for Immersive and 3D Visual Media (including Visual Quality Assessment, Visual Attention and Saliency Segmentation, Restoration and Enhancement, 3D Object Detection)
  • Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence:
    Machine Learning/Deep Learning and Optimization Methods, Interpretable Deep Learning, Light-weight Deep Neural Networks for Multimedia and AI Accelerators (HW/SW, VLSI/Embedded Hardware Design)
