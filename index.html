Biography

    Dr. Wei GAO is an Assistant Professor (Ph.D. Supervisor) with School of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Shenzhen Graduate School, Peking University (PKU), Shenzhen, China. He is now leading the Visual Perception & Immersive Computing Team (VPIC, 视觉感知与沉浸式计算课题组). He received the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), Kowloon, Hong Kong. He was a Camera ISP Engineer at OmniVision Technologies (OVT), Shanghai, China. He was a Visiting Scholar at Electrical Engineering Department, University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), CA, USA. He worked as Postdoctoral Fellow and Research Associate at Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), Kowloon, Hong Kong, and Research Fellow at School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, respectively. His research interests include Multimedia Coding and Processing, Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence. Moreover, theoretical research on applied mathematics and optimization, visual perception, aritifical intelligence and practical information systems are also very interesting. He believes that: good innovations should have good explainable theories. Our VPIC team endeavors to improve the visual experience and industrial applications of immersive and 3D visual media, and thus boost the advancements of emerging and future multimedia and visual information processing technology.

    高伟博士是北京大学信息工程学院助理教授/副研究员/博士生导师，深圳市海外高层次人才。香港城市大学计算机科学博士，曾在美国加州大学洛杉矶分校（UCLA）做访问学者，曾在香港城市大学和新加坡南洋理工大学做博士后研究，曾在CMOS图像传感器制造商OmniVision Technologies公司担任工程师。研究方向为沉浸式与3D视觉媒体信息处理技术（点云、光场、全景、多视点/双目3D等），主要研究兴趣包括：（1）多媒体编码与处理：视频与点云编码、深度学习智能编码；沉浸式与3D视觉媒体的感知质量评价、视觉注意机制与显著性/3D目标检测、复原增强；（2）深度学习与人工智能：机器学习/深度学习与优化理论及应用、可解释性深度神经网络理论、人工智能中深度学习算法轻量化/结构搜索与软硬件加速。近年来主要科研成果发表在相关领域高水平国际期刊（如IEEE TIP、TCSVT、TMM、TIM、TCYB、TII等）和高水平国际会议（如AAAI、ACM MM等）上40余篇，申请或授权美国/中国/PCT专利30余项，积极参与多媒体与人工智能技术的标准提案工作。曾荣获2019年和2020年连续两年的CCF-腾讯犀牛鸟基金获得者、2019年广东省计算机学会优秀论文一等奖，2021年CCF-腾讯犀牛鸟优秀专利奖，并由于在3D沉浸式媒体方面的研究荣获ICME 2021多媒体学术新星奖（Multimedia Rising Star）。国家自然科学基金、广东省与深圳市项目评审专家。担任机器学习与多媒体计算领域国际重要SCI期刊副编辑（Associate Editor），包括Neural Processing Letters（2021-）、IET Image Processing（2020-）、Electronics Letters（2020-），同时是众多国际顶级期刊IEEE TIP、TCSVT、TMM、TNNLS、TCYB等的审稿人，多个国际学术会议程序委员会委员与组织方（ICME 2021等）。近年来，作为项目负责人主持10余项国家级与省部级等重大/重要科研项目，包括主持科技部项目（课题）2项（科技创新2030-新一代人工智能重大项目课题1项，科技部-XXX项目课题1项）、国家自然科学基金项目（课题）2项（重点项目课题1项，青年项目1项）、广东省自然科学基金项目1项（面上项目1项）、深圳市基础研究项目2项（重点项目1项，面上项目1项）、腾讯公司委托项目3项等。作为研究骨干参与国家自然科学基金3项、香港研究资助局优配研究基金1项、香港创新科技署项目1项等。课题组致力于提升沉浸式与3D视觉媒体的观看体验与工业应用，促进新兴与未来多媒体与视觉信息处理技术发展。

Research

    Our research areas are Multimedia Coding and Processing, and Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence.
  • Multimedia Coding and Processing: 2D/3D/Perceptual Video Coding, Point Cloud Compression, AI-Based Media Compression for Immersive and 3D Visual Media Communication (Point Cloud, Light Field, Panorama, Multiview/Binocular 3D for VR/AR); Visual Quality Assessment, Visual Attention and Salient/3D Object Detection, Restoration and Enhancement for Immersive and 3D Visual Media
  • Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence: Machine Learning/Deep Learning and Optimization Methods, Interpretable Deep Learning, Light-weight Deep Neural Network/Neural Architecture Search for Multimedia and AI Accelerators (HW/SW, VLSI/Embedded Design)
